Sabarmati River Clean-Up

Ahmedabad, Gujarat  ·  Every Sunday, 7:00 AM

About This Initiative

Weekly volunteer-driven campaign to remove plastic waste, industrial debris and other pollutants from the Sabarmati riverbank. Started in 2022, this initiative has removed over 12 tonnes of waste.

Goals
  • Remove 20 tonnes of waste from the riverbank this year
  • Engage 500+ unique volunteers
  • Partner with 10 local schools for awareness programs
